CraftTracker: Microbusiness Expense Tracker

Rapid prototyping of a conceptual application.

Craft Tracker is a conceptual application that helps creative entrepreneurs manage their inventory and expenses.

I completed this project while studing at DePaul on a team of three students.

Prototype V1 - Conceptual Evaluation

For this project, we completed a new design and testing cycle each week. Each prototype built incrementally upon the last. We used axure to build and host the prototypes. It allowed us to build in interactivity as well as designs for testing.

Our first prototype focused on one basic feature: adding an expense by scanning a receipt.

Value Testing

We tested the interactive prototype on six potential users to confirm the need for this type of application in the target audience. Our testers strongly felt that this application would be useful.

Prototype V2 - Task Flow Refinement

During our first round of testing we discovered that the flow through the receipt scanning had some extra, unnecessary steps.

In our second prototype, we simplified the receipt scanning steps and added stronger confirmation.

Prototype V3 - Responsive Views

After our first round of iteration and testing we began creating tablet and desktop views of the application. We also began creating the and feel of the design.

usability Testing

We tested the high-fideltity, interactive prototype on six potential users to identify usability issues. We completed the test with 6 participants, 2 participants per view.

We used the findings from this test to create a "final" polished axure prototype.

Prototype V4 - Final, High-Fidelity Prototype

In our final round of testing we found that overall our participants were excited about the design idea. However, in some views they had difficulty finding the "Add Materials" button. They also felt that application was too "dry" and wanted it to be more fun and engaging to use.

Home page, add materials can be accessed by tray on bottom or in the hamburger menu.

Add tray open, user can now select the camera to begin scanning a receipt.

Items scanned from receipt, user can edit or delete before confirming.

Confirmation screen - we added a more "fun" message and icons to increase the playfulness of the app.

After confirmation, the user is returned to the desktop, which now has a running list of their materials.
